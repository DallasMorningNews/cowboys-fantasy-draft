TEAM OF STARS

Construct your dream team that spans the history of the Dallas Cowboys

By SportsDay

Published September 10, 2015

    Nostalgia or numbers. That's the question you need to answer when building your fantasy Dallas Cowboys team. Sure, you can go with guys like Tony Romo and Dez Bryant, who would rack up the points in a modern-day fantasy-football lineup. But which legendary quarterback would you want leading your ultimate Cowboys dream team? Troy Aikman has a few flashy reasons you might want to consider. So spend a couple of minutes picking your fantasy team, but remember that the budget is limited. You have $18 to spend on six positions — don't worry, the kicker is free — so choose wisely. Let us know your dream lineup in the comments below, and make sure to share with your friends on Facebook and Twitter.

    Add players from each position to your roster using the button. If you change your mind on one of your picks, remove them from your roster using the button. Pick one quarterback, two running backs, two receivers, one defensive unit and one kicker. When you're done, submit your draft to see how often your players have been selected by other readers.
